What do we do?

Do you feel tired while studying, working or any other activities that require your full attention?

And feel energetic at moments you don't have anything to do? With this app you will be able to predict when you will have your best performance and optimize your daily routine.

Unsure if you have time to do daily tests?

These tests require only a couple of minutes on several moments of your day, but you will easily get this time back through optimized time management.

Do cognitive tests sound boring to you?

All tests have been gamified. So have fun playing the games and we do the analytics for you. We add new games reguraly, so you will never get bored. Just sit back and relax!

How do you know we are reliable?

Our tests and analysis is based on the scientific research of many univerities and scientists. We keep our test up-to-date to the latest findings.
